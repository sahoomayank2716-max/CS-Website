Govt. Digvijay PG Autonomous College

About the College

Established in 1957, Government Digvijay Autonomous Post Graduate College, Rajnandgaon, stands as a pillar of higher education with a legacy that spans over six decades. Founded under the visionary patronage of Raja Digvijay Das, the college has become one of the premier institutions in Chhattisgarh.

The college reflects the rich cultural heritage of Rajnandgaon while embracing modern educational practices. Renowned Hindi literary figures like Pt. Padum Lal Punna Lal Bakshi, Gajanand Madhav Muktibodh, and Pt. Baldeo Prasad Mishra contributed to its legacy.

Accredited as B++ by NAAC, the college serves students from rural and tribal backgrounds and promotes women empowerment. It was among the first institutions to implement NEP 2020.

The college enrolls over 6000 students across 1000+ courses in various streams including Arts, Commerce, Science, Computer Applications, and more.

With advanced labs and strong research culture, the college has 11 patents and 300+ research publications. It has also secured research grants worth Rs 90 lakhs.

Facilities include digital classrooms, laboratories, auditorium, internships, and placement opportunities for students.

The college also offers distance education through IGNOU and Pt. Sundarlal Sharma Open University.

It has a strong sports culture with achievers like Gyaneshwari Yadav, Mrinal Chaubey, and Ajay Mandal representing at national and international levels.

NCC and NSS units actively promote discipline, leadership, and social service among students.

The college continues to be a center of excellence in academics, sports, and overall development.
